Our Services

Smart Elephant Detection & Instant Alerts

WildEye harnesses the power of AI with YOLOv8 to accurately detect elephants in real time, helping prevent human-wildlife conflicts. Upon detection, the system instantly sends automated email alerts to notify users, enabling quick action to protect lives and property. With high accuracy and rapid response, WildEye ensures a reliable and proactive solution for wildlife monitoring and safety.

Web-Based Monitoring

WildEye provides a user-friendly web-based monitoring system that allows users to track elephant detections in real time. The intuitive dashboard displays live detection events, past records, and alert history, ensuring complete visibility and control. Accessible from any device, the system enables proactive decision-making to enhance safety and wildlife conservation efforts.

Detection History

WildEye logs all detection events, providing valuable insights into elephant movement patterns. This historical data helps improve response strategies and supports wildlife conservation efforts.

Cross-Device Accessibility & Responsive UI

WildEye is designed to be fully responsive, ensuring a seamless experience across all devices—desktops, tablets, and smartphones. Whether you're at home or on the move, you can access real-time alerts and monitoring effortlessly.

User-Friendly Authentication System

Our secure and efficient login and registration system ensures seamless access to the WildEye platform. With a smooth authentication process, users can quickly sign in and view alert history with ease.
